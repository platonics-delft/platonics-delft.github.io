About me

While my research focus in applied differential geometry for trajectory generation, my passion for software development and my eager to compete with the best researchers in competitive robotics has led me join the Platonics.

During my Ph.D. @ TU Delft, I have been working mainly on model predictive control and geometric fabrics for motion planning. Due to the project funding, I applied the developed methods for retail environments.
